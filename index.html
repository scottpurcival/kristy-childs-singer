    About Me

    The Voice Behind
    The Music

    Hi, I'm Kristy Childs — a passionate singer and songwriter with a heart full of music that blends the honest storytelling of country with the infectious energy of pop.

    Music has always been my way of connecting with the world. Whether it's a quiet ballad that captures an unspoken feeling, or an upbeat anthem that gets you moving, I pour everything into every single note. My songs are drawn from real life — love, loss, joy, and everything in between.

    I've been performing and honing my craft for years, and I can't wait to share more of my journey with you. Stay tuned for new music, live shows, and everything that's still to come!
